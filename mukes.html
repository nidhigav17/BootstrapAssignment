Mukesh Ambani

Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani is an Indian billionaire businessman. He is currently the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries, India's most valuable company by market value. With an estimated net worth of $92 billion as of December 2023, he is the richest person in Asia and 15th richest in the world.
